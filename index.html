Motto

Knowledge is power, but Bitcoin is the future.

Mission

To empower the kaka (men) community across Africa through financial literacy and Bitcoin education, enabling them to harness the transformative power of Bitcoin. We aim to provide tailored workshops and resources that teach essential skills in digital finance, entrepreneurship, and blockchain technology. By equipping boys with the knowledge and tools to engage in the global economy, we strive to foster a generation of financial education and the role of Bitcoin and empower boys to achieve economic independence and leadership, who can drive positive change in their communities through Bitcoin.

Vision

We strive to foster a generation of financial education and the role of Bitcoin and empower boys to achieve economic independence and leadership, who can drive positive change in their communities through Bitcoin.

Goals

  • Financial Empowerment: Equip men with the knowledge and skills to achieve financial independence through Bitcoin and cryptocurrency investments.
  • Digital Literacy: Increase digital literacy and understanding of blockchain technology within the target community.
  • Economic Growth: Foster economic growth and development within African communities through Bitcoin-related entrepreneurship and innovation.
  • Community Building: Create a strong and supportive network of men engaged in the Bitcoin space.
  • Social Impact: Promote positive social change by empowering men to contribute to their communities and improve their lives.

Values

  • Education: Prioritize education and knowledge sharing as the foundation for empowerment.
  • Integrity: Maintain transparency, honesty, and ethical conduct in all community activities.
  • Inclusivity: Foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, regardless of their background or experience.
